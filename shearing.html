a Hadley cell may be considered as a composition of radially (on earth, meridional) overturning circulation and circumferential (o.e. zonal) flows that vary with depth.
As the green dye tracer falls with gravity and input forcing and is carried with and stretched most rapidly by the cyclonic flows of the upper fluid. How do you think the experiment would have looked if we put the dye drops closer to the cold center? Further away? Would there be any _bad_ places to drop the dye? Any best?

The turntable is spun counterclockwise to model the northern hemisphere. To model the southern hemisphere, the tank would be rotated clockwise; this is the same west to east direction spin viewed from below. The turntable models the middle latitude (extra-tropical) zone with rotation speeds @ ~10rpm. Lower latitude tropical regions, we spin the table @ ~1rpm At the equator, the direction of gravity is effectively perpendicular to the earth's axis of rotation. The effect of the planet's rotation is entirely in the vertical component of air motion, where hydrostatic forces dominate.
